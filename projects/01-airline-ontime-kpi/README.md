# Airline On-Time Performance KPI (Case Study)

## Business Goal
Measure airline operational performance and identify delay/cancellation drivers to surface improvement opportunities.

## KPIs (examples)
- On-time rate
- Average delay (minutes)
- Cancellation rate
- Diversion rate
- Top delay reasons (if available)

- SQL queries: `sql/`
- Executive summary: `reports/executive_summary.md`
